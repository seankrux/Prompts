# Multimodal CoT Prompting

Source: https://www.promptingguide.ai/techniques/multimodalcot

## Overview

Zhang et al. (2023) introduced "a multimodal chain-of-thought prompting approach" that extends traditional reasoning techniques beyond text alone. Rather than focusing exclusively on language, this method integrates both textual and visual information within a structured two-stage framework.

## Two-Stage Framework

1. **Rationale Generation** - Creates explanatory reasoning based on combined visual and textual data
2. **Answer Inference** - Uses the generated reasoning to arrive at conclusions

## Key Finding

A 1 billion-parameter multimodal CoT model demonstrated superior performance compared to GPT-3.5 on the ScienceQA benchmark.
